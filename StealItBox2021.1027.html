Is the Steal It Box a subscription box?

No! We are a no commitment, hassle free quarterly box that does not require a subscription. The only thing you need to do is come back every season to purchase your next box! Stay informed on upcoming box purchase dates by subscribing to our email and SMS text message alerts.

Can I choose what is in my box?

Each season’s box is carefully curated in advance and there are no variations among boxes. All boxes per season include the same items. If you buy more than one, you will receive identical boxes. Currently we do not offer the option for you to be able to pick items or exclude items.

I saw one of your ads, is that what will be in my box?

Our ads show a mixture of past products as well as styling props. We do not always feature the items that will be in your Steal It Box. Keep in mind that these boxes are a surprise so revealing everything would ruin the fun!

My box and/or items arrived damaged. What should I do?

We are SO sorry to hear you received a damaged or incomplete box! If your order has arrived damaged or your box is incomplete, be sure to notify us within 30 days of the original ship date in order to be eligible for a refund or replacement. Please include photos of both the item and the packaging it came in (be sure to save your box)! These are required for our team to be able to assist you. This also helps us understand what went wrong so we can prevent similar issues for you and other customers in the future. To get started click Click HERE to request an RMA! and select “Damaged or Missing Item”.

What's the return policy?

We are so sorry to hear that you didn’t love the items in your box! We strive to make items that will go well with everyone’s style! Since these items are carefully chosen and the boxes are priced well below retail, we are unable to do returns or substitutes for anything in your box. If you do not like a particular item, we always recommend gifting to a friend, family member, or donating the item(s)! Add-on shop item returns are accepted within 30 days of the date the item was sent to you, as long as the item is unused, accompanied by a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number, and returned in its original packaging. Make sure to hang on to your box until you are sure you LOVE your items. Click HERE to request an RMA!
